WHAT IS NEXATHON'25?

Nexathon 2025, a 24-hour national-level hackathon organized by Nexus Tech Club of VIT Chennai, scheduled for February 10-11, 2025. Nexathon focuses on the Open Innovation Challenge, providing a platform for creative minds to ideate, innovate, and solve real-world problems. This event will bring together students, developers, and tech enthusiasts from across India.
